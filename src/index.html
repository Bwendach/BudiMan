About the Story

Enter a world where everyone develops superpowers at the age of 19 - everyone except our hero.

In a world where supernatural abilities are the norm, Budiman stands out as the exception - a college student with no powers in a campus full of gifted individuals.

When his best friend Bryan (who possesses comedic but limited fart powers) is kidnapped by the electric-wielding villain Willsen, Budiman must find courage beyond superpowers.

In a moment of crisis, Budiman discovers an unexpected ability - the power to stop time. But will it be enough to save his friend and confront the shocking betrayal that awaits?

About the Production

"Budi Man" is a short film created by talented students from Binus University as part of their final project. The film explores themes of heroism, friendship, and finding strength in unexpected places.
